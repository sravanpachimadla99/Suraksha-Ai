# SurakshaAI - Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title:** SurakshaAI
- **Tagline:** Predict. Prevent. Protect. AI-Powered Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform
- **Visual:** High-tech modern cybersecurity graphic

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Current State:** Law enforcement is reactive. Citizens report fraud *after* losing money.
- **Pain Points:** 
  - Digital Arrest Scams are rising.
  - Counterfeit currency is hard to detect by the public.
  - Fraud networks are highly organized.

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **SurakshaAI:** A proactive, AI-first intelligence platform.
- **Value Proposition:** Shifts policing from reactive investigation to predictive prevention. 

## Slide 4: Key Modules (The "How")
- **Citizen Fraud Shield:** AI assistant for calls, SMS, and URLs.
- **Counterfeit Detection:** CV-based currency scanning.
- **Fraud Network Intelligence:** Graph AI to expose mule accounts.
- **Geospatial Intelligence:** GIS dashboard for predicting hot spots.

## Slide 5: System Architecture
- **Tech Stack Overview:** Mobile-first (Next.js), Cloud-native (FastAPI), Scalable DBs (Neo4j, Postgres).
- **AI Models:** YOLOv8, Whisper, Gemini, Graph Neural Networks.

## Slide 6: Demo Overview
- Briefly introduce the demo scenario: Citizen receives a fake "Digital Arrest" call -> AI detects -> Police alerted -> Network graphed.

## Slide 7: Business & Social Impact
- **Impact:** Preventing millions in financial loss, restoring trust in digital systems.
- **Scalability:** Built on cloud infrastructure to deploy state-wide and nationally.

## Slide 8: Future Roadmap
- Integration with Telecom providers at the network level.
- Deepfake video detection for election security.
- Advanced multilingual support across 22+ languages.
